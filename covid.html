OUR COVID-19 RESPONSE

The first thing we did was close our doors to public walk in service to have a staff meeting and get off the merry go round of panic buying and mad purchasing and overworking ourselves. We decided to stop running and catch our breath. We decided to gather our energy to walk this out and re-strategize. Finally we decided to serve our awesome customers in new ways like contactless delivery and contactless pick up while we renovated the store and reorganized and retrained our staff for safety and the potential for months of social isolation. Thank you for all our loyal customers who were patient in this process. Below are the results of our labour.

STEPS WE HAVE TAKEN

We have taken many measures to ensure that we can still bring you top quality natural products in the safest way possible!

Call Centre

We have added a call centre for contactless delivery! It is free for seniors or $5 for a 10km radius. The call centre also handles preorder and pick up requests so you can get in and out of the store as fast as possible or pick up curbside!

Sanitation Measures

We have installed a heavy plexiglass shield for cashiers, added hand sanitizing stations, developed regular disinfection routines, and provided necessary PPE for our staff.

Customer Limits

We have established a limit of 4 customers in the store at any one time. Customers can wait in the designated spots outside the store if needed. We have also rearranged the flow and traffic inside the store to maintain social distance and removed any open samples.

Staff Care

We are providing our staff with bonuses during this difficult time, and ensuring that no symptomatic staff are allowed to come to work. Staff are separated into groups so that front line staff don't interact with call centre staff.

We offer public walk-in shopping, contactless delivery and prepay/pick-up in store or curbside.

HOW TO ORDER PRODUCTS

To place an order, you can contact our call centre at (416) 698-8805 to request products or information. Because of high call volume, you may need to leave a message with your name and phone number and our call centre will get back to you either the same business day or the next business day.

When we call you back we are ready to serve you live on the phone and will pick and organize your order on the spot.

You can pre-pay for orders by credit card over the phone.

CONTACTLESS DELIVERY

Contactless delivery is free of charge for seniors and $5 for anyone living within a 10km radius of our store. Added delivery charges are for orders beyond 10km.

Contactless Delivery is every Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday late afternoons or evenings

PICK-UP ORDERS

Pick up orders are curbside (just knock on the door) OR enter the store and ask.

Curbside pick up is only available during our REGULAR STORE HOURS (see public walk in hours) and after hours on Tuesdays from 6pm-8pm.
