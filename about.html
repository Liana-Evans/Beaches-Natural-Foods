ABOUT US

We are a family and Canadian owned business that started in the west end of Toronto in 1997 and has expanded to the east end. Our Beaches location was opened in December of 2002. We love it in the Beaches! Great neighbourhood and customers! We have customers from all over the city and even outside Toronto that we enjoy serving. Our reviews show that our customers in turn appreciate our positive atmosphere, great staff and quality products at discount prices. We believe in adapting, adjusting and changing to serve our loyal customers better so the best is yet to come! With that in mind, we are happy and excited to be setting up an online store! Stay tuned for details.

MEET THE OWNERS

Wallace Chau (Far left)

Wallace Chau started Beaches Natural Foods in December of 2002. He is a graduate of Queen’s University. Wallace worked at his parent’s health food store in the west end of Toronto for a number of years before deciding to open his own store in the Beaches area. He has a natural affinity for supplement knowledge, creative ideas and is well experienced and connected in purchasing and buying in the health food industry. Customers enjoy his friendly and helpful nature and wealth of experience.

Alek Chau (Second from right)

Alek Chau spent a year helping to renovate Beaches Natural Foods before assisting his brother, Wallace to open the store in December of 2002. He graduated from Queen’s University before going to study philosophy at the University of Toronto for two years. He then obtained a B. Ed. degree from Lakehead University in Thunder Bay and taught in the public school system for over a decade. He is also a pastor at the Messianic Church of God. Alek believes in living a life that is honourable to God, in service to people and being the best and highest you can possibly be.
